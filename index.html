Reading & Language Arts

Henry was a boy who loved to read. In our weekly story, we are learning how to find the "Main Idea" of a paragraph. When you read a story, look for the topic sentence.

Next, we will look at "Supporting Details." These are the facts that help prove the main idea. For example, if the story is about how dogs make great pets, a detail might be that dogs are very loyal.

Unit 4: Fractions and Decimals

When adding fractions with unlike denominators, you must find the LCM.

$$\frac{1}{2} + \frac{1}{3} = \frac{5}{6}$$

This Week's Goals:

  • Multiply decimals by powers of ten.
  • Divide multi-digit whole numbers.
  • Identify prime and composite numbers.
